The Web Designer

A story of HTML, CSS, and the big city

She knew HTML. She knew CSS. But did she know love? Read this destined-to-be-remaindered novel that The New York Times Book Review described as “reasonably grammatical” and the Times Literary Supplement called “bathroom-worthy.” Pre-order your paperback copy now for just $14.99.

At High Falutin High, the arts high school in her home town, Daisy Fontana fell in love. Not with a boy, or even with a girl, for that matter, but with something altogether more interesting: web design. Instead of a BFF, she had CSS. Instead of singing and dancing with the other kids, she spent her time coding alone. But when she graduated and moved to the city to find a job, she knew everything about HTML, but nothing about life. Will the town eat her alive, or will she survive and rise to the top of the cutthroat world of coding websites? Daisy Fontana is a nerd heroine for our times, and The Web Designer tells her gripping tale.
